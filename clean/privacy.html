Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-13

Android Clean ("the App") is an Android application developed by Niko Digital that scans your device's storage to help you find and remove junk files (cache, temporary files, logs) and duplicate files. This policy explains what information the App handles and how.

Data collected

The App collects no personal data. It does not create accounts, does not require sign-in, and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information.

How your files are used

When you run a scan, the App reads file metadata (such as name, size, type, and modification date) and computes content hashes to identify duplicates. All scanning and analysis happen entirely on your device. The App has no network functionality and makes no internet connections, so your files and their contents never leave your device.

File deletion

Deleting files is always initiated by you. The App only removes files you select and confirm for deletion, and it shows you exactly what will be removed beforehand. The App does not modify or delete anything without your explicit action.

Data sharing

No data is shared with third parties. The App contains no analytics libraries, no advertising SDKs, and no crash-reporting services. Nothing is uploaded, tracked, or shared.

Data stored on device

The App does not maintain a persistent database of your files. Scan results are held only in memory while the App is running and are discarded when you close it. You can remove all App data at any time by clearing the App's data in Android Settings, or by uninstalling the App.

Permissions

  • Storage access (read / manage external storage) — used to scan the files on your device so junk and duplicates can be identified, and to delete the files you choose to remove. File access is used only on your device for these purposes.
  • Delete cache files — used to clear app cache that the system reports as reclaimable, only when you choose to clean it.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. It does not knowingly collect any information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If the privacy practices change, the updated policy will be published at this URL and the effective date above will be updated.
